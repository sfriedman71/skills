# Incubator

Experimental skills, draft workflows, scenarios, and design notes.

Content here is public to show process, but it is **not** released or supported for installation. Released skills live in `../skills/`.

**Draft `SKILL.md` files in this directory must not be symlinked into OpenCode or any skill loader.** They are unfinished and may change or be removed without notice.

## Layout

```text
incubator/<skill-name>/
  README.md
  SKILL.md
```

Use each incubator skill's `README.md` for the idea, intended triggers, scenarios, notes, and promotion checklist.

## Disclaimer

Incubator content is not audited, not install-supported, and may be incomplete or unsafe to execute. Use at your own risk.
